Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior dance experience? +

Not at all. We welcome absolute beginners as well as professional dancers. Guru Gobind tailors his instruction to every skill level.

What should I wear to class? +

Comfortable, flexible clothing. For traditional classes, you may be asked to wear ethnic attire. Bare feet or soft dance shoes are recommended.

How long is each class? +

Group sessions typically run 60–90 minutes. Private lessons are 60 minutes. Assessment consultations are approximately 30 minutes.

Is there parking available? +

Street parking is available around 76th St. The studio is also easily accessible via the 7 train (Jackson Heights–Roosevelt Ave station).
